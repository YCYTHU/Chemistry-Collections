# Hybrid orbitals

## $\bf D_{\infty h}$ (Linear)

$$sp\text{ hybridization}$$

$$
  \begin{bmatrix}
   h_1\\
   h_2
  \end{bmatrix}=
  \begin{bmatrix}
   \frac{1}{\sqrt2} & \frac{1}{\sqrt2}\\
   \frac{1}{\sqrt2} & -\frac{1}{\sqrt2}
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
   s\\
   p_z
  \end{bmatrix}
$$

## $\bf D_{3h}$ (Trigonal planar)

$$sp^2\text{ hybridization}$$

$$
  \begin{bmatrix}
   h_1\\
   h_2\\
   h_3
  \end{bmatrix}=
  \begin{bmatrix}
  \frac{1}{\sqrt3} & \sqrt{\frac23} & 0\\
  \frac{1}{\sqrt3} & -\frac{1}{\sqrt6} & \frac{1}{\sqrt2}\\
  \frac{1}{\sqrt3} & -\frac{1}{\sqrt6} & -\frac{1}{\sqrt2}
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
   s\\
   p_x\\
   p_y
  \end{bmatrix}
$$

## $\bf T_d$ (Tetrahedron)

$$sp^3\text{ hybridization}$$
$$
  \begin{bmatrix}
   h_1\\
   h_2\\
   h_3\\
   h_4
  \end{bmatrix}=
  \begin{bmatrix}
  \frac12 & \frac12 & \frac12 & \frac12\\
  \frac12 & -\frac12 & -\frac12 & \frac12\\
  \frac12 & \frac12 & -\frac12 & -\frac12\\
  \frac12 & -\frac12 & \frac12 & -\frac12\\  
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
   s\\
   p_x\\
   p_y\\
   p_z
  \end{bmatrix}
$$

$$sd^3\text{ hybridization}$$
$$
  \begin{bmatrix}
   h_1\\
   h_2\\
   h_3\\
   h_4
  \end{bmatrix}=
  \begin{bmatrix}
  \frac12 & \frac12 & \frac12 & \frac12\\
  \frac12 & -\frac12 & -\frac12 & \frac12\\
  \frac12 & \frac12 & -\frac12 & -\frac12\\
  \frac12 & -\frac12 & \frac12 & -\frac12\\  
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
   s\\
   d_{xz}\\
   d_{yz}\\
   d_{xy}
  \end{bmatrix}
$$
