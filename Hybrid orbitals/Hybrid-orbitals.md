# Hybrid orbitals

## $sp\text{ hybridization (Linear)}$

$$
  \begin{bmatrix}
   h_1\\
   h_2
  \end{bmatrix}=
  \begin{bmatrix}
   \frac{1}{\sqrt2} & \frac{1}{\sqrt2}\\
   \frac{1}{\sqrt2} & -\frac{1}{\sqrt2}
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
   s\\
   p_z
  \end{bmatrix}
$$

## $sp^3d^2\text{ hybridization (Octahedral)}$

$$
  \begin{bmatrix}
   h_1\\
   h_2\\
   h_3\\
   h_4\\
   h_5\\
   h_6
  \end{bmatrix}=
  \begin{bmatrix}
  \frac{1}{\sqrt6} & \frac{1}{\sqrt2} & 0 & 0 & -\frac{1}{\sqrt{12}} & \frac12\\
  \frac{1}{\sqrt6} & -\frac{1}{\sqrt2} & 0 & 0 & -\frac{1}{\sqrt{12}} & \frac12\\ 
  \frac{1}{\sqrt6} & 0 & \frac{1}{\sqrt2} & 0 & -\frac{1}{\sqrt{12}} & -\frac12\\
  \frac{1}{\sqrt6} & 0 & -\frac{1}{\sqrt2} & 0 & -\frac{1}{\sqrt{12}} & -\frac12\\
  \frac{1}{\sqrt6} & 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{\sqrt2} & \frac{2}{\sqrt{12}} & 0\\
  \frac{1}{\sqrt6} & 0 & 0 & -\frac{1}{\sqrt2} & \frac{2}{\sqrt{12}} & 0
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
   s\\
   p_x\\
   p_y\\
   p_z\\
   d_{z^2}\\
   d_{x^2-y^2}
  \end{bmatrix}
$$
